App Category: Board, Puzzle, Strategy

App Description: The 15 Puzzle is a sliding 4x4 tile puzzle. This game is inspired by the A* Star Algorithm, an algorithm that can be used to find the shortest path of a winning board. The objective of the game is to order the tiles from 1-15 horizontally. Whenever a tile is in its correct position, it will turn green. Turn all the tiles green to win!
